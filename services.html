Our services

1

Tailor Sweing

At Horlar Stitches, we take sewing seriously. Every stitch matters, and we make sure your clothes fit just right. Our team knows all about fabrics and uses their skills to make sure you look great in what we make for you. If you want a special outfit that fits like it was made just for you, we're here to make it happen.

2

Mesurement

Getting the right fit starts with the right measurements. At Horlar Stitches, we do more than just use a tape measure. We look at how you stand and move to make sure your clothes will be comfortable and look good. Come in for a fitting and see how our careful measuring makes all the difference.

3

Ready-made

Need something stylish fast? Check out our ready-made clothes. We have lots of styles to choose from, all made with care and quality, just like our custom pieces. These are great if you need something quickly or just want to freshen up your wardrobe without waiting for custom sewing.

Why use our service?

Horlar Stitches is dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of tailoring services to meet all your wardrobe needs:.

Bespoke Tailoring

Our bespoke tailoring service is the epitome of luxury and personalization. Whether you desire a perfectly fitted suit, an elegant dress, or a stylish pair of trousers, our skilled artisans will work closely with you to bring your vision to life. From selecting the finest fabrics to meticulously crafting every detail, we ensure that each piece is tailored to your exact specifications, reflecting your unique style and personality.

Alterations

Sometimes, your favorite garments need a little tweak to achieve the perfect fit. Our alteration service is here to help! Whether it's adjusting the hem of your trousers, taking in the waist of your dress, or resizing your suit jacket, our expert tailors will perform precision alterations to enhance the fit and comfort of your existing garments. Trust us to breathe new life into your wardrobe favorites.

Special Occasions

Your special day deserves special attire. At Horlar Stitches, we specialize in creating stunning wedding attire, prom dresses, and formal wear that will make you look and feel extraordinary. Whether you envision a timeless and classic wedding gown, a dashing tuxedo for your groomsmen, or a glamorous prom dress that steals the spotlight, our team will work tirelessly to ensure that your big day is as memorable as can be.

Wardrobe Consultation

Not sure where to start when it comes to building your wardrobe? Let our experienced stylists guide you! Our wardrobe consultation service offers professional advice to help you curate a wardrobe that reflects your individuality and suits your lifestyle. From choosing the right fabrics and colors to selecting cuts that flatter your figure, we'll help you make informed decisions that enhance your personal style and confidence.
